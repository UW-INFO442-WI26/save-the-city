# User Personas — Save the City

## Persona 1: Maria Lopez (Garden Host)

**Age:** 40  
**Occupation:** Elementary School Teacher  
**Location:** South Seattle  
**Role:** Garden Host  

**Description:**  
Maria helps manage a small community garden right by her school. She wants an easy way to share what produce is available and organize volunteer help, since she currently relies on word of mouth and flyers. She is comfortable using basic apps but does not want anything complicated. She would use the platform to register her garden, post volunteer times, and communicate with people interested in helping or harvesting produce.

**Goals:**
- Register her garden so others can find it
- Recruit volunteers to help maintain the garden
- Share when produce is available

**Frustrations:**
- Hard to reach people who want to volunteer
- No centralized place to post garden information
- Current communication methods are inefficient

## Persona 2: Jamal Carter (Consumer / Volunteer)

**Age:** 24  
**Occupation:** Warehouse Worker  
**Location:** West Seattle  
**Role:** Consumer / Volunteer  

**Description:**  
Jamal lives in an area with limited access to fresh produce and wants healthier food options. He does not own a car, so he depends on nearby resources. He is comfortable using mobile apps and would use the platform to find nearby community gardens, see what produce is available, and sign up to volunteer or harvest food.

**Goals:**
- Find nearby community gardens
- Access fresh produce close to home
- Volunteer and participate in his community

**Frustrations:**
- Grocery stores with fresh produce are far away
- Doesn’t know where community gardens are located
- Limited visibility into available food resources
